FAQ

1. Registration of the settlement is given by the guest giving a passport to the community and a 100% payment for the entire period of staying in Verholy Relax Park hotels (far hotels).
2. The hour of arrival (settlement) is about 15:00, the hour of arrival is about 12:00 after the first hour.
3. At the end of the visit from the declared date, the sum of the non-corroded service of the guest does not receive compensation.
4. Payment for overbooking is carried out at an affordable rate per hour for overbooking in a hotel for the entire application period.
